About Stillwater

Stillwater, located in north-central Oklahoma, is a vibrant college town known for being the home of Oklahoma State University. With a rich agricultural history and a strong connection to music and culture, Stillwater offers a unique blend of small-town charm and academic excellence.

Fun Facts

  • Stillwater is known as the birthplace of Red Dirt music.
  • Home to Oklahoma State University, a major educational and research institution.
  • The city has a strong agricultural background and is a hub for innovation in agriculture.
